Social Track

Welcome to SOCIAL TRACK, a competitive platform created by EDC JSS and ATTAC Society ,in collaboration with Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of Uttar Pradesh. Social Track competition at The Entrepreneurship Summit Noida will be an intensive coding and product development competition which aims to develop products fueled by most competent product developers and teams at JSSATEN. The products could then be implemented on large scale by the government, and would serve the participants as their most valuable profile additives over their student life at JSSATEN.

Incentives

  • Cash prizes worth 20,000 INR.
  • Certificate of Merit by Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of UP, ATTAC NGO and EDC JSS.
  • Oppurtunity to pitch to District Magistrate, Chief Medical Officer, inverstors, senior doctors and founder of YouWeCan.
  • Goodies by Creators Gurukul.

Event Structure

  • All participants are required to fill the abstracts, on basis of problem statements (click link), before Wednesday, 18 April 2018, IST 23:59 hrs
  • The documents will be submitted in the form (click link) mandatorily before the deadline.
  • The declaration of Top 15 teams will be done on Thursday, 19 April 2018, as judged by the panel of doctors and experienced entrepreneurs.
  • The finalist teams will be required to present a working prototype of the solution on Saturday, 21 April 2018 in front of the panel of judges having senior-level government officials, senior doctors, CMO, investors, etc.
  • Furthermore, the teams submitting their abstracts are advised to start working before the declaration of finalists, in order to comfortably be ready with their working prototypes by the Finals on Saturday, 21 April 2018.
  • Entries need to have an innovative value (an innovative idea provides a solution for a new need, or advancement compared to existing products, services or technologies).

GuideLines

  • The numbers of members in a team should range from 4 to a maximum of 6 members per team.
  • Students (undergraduates and graduates) studying at JSSATE Noida are eligible to apply however team members may be from different academic levels.
  • Entries must be the original work of participants i.e. team members competing must be the principal developers (both the technical and management) of the venture.
  • The solutions proposed and developed must correspond to one of the problem statements given by the organizers.
  • Each team will be allotted an experienced doctor who could mentor the team on medical aspects.
  • In case of any discrepancies, the final decision making authority lies with Social Track - Organizing Committee.
