What is E-SUMMIT?

The Entrepreneurship Summit Noida is a one stop destination for the youth community, venture capitalists, new age entrepreneurs and people passionate for entrepreneurship to engage in activities that foster entrepreneurial spirit and encourage people to take initiatives and actions in their own lives.

VISIT OUR FACEBOOK PAGE

Major events of the Summit

Speaker Session

Speakers were invited to share their journey and life experiences. Each speaker had a different background and varied age group.

Internship Fair

Two day internship fair was organized. The internships were offered for various roles in Mechanical engineering, Electrical Engineering, Project management, Business Development, Marketing, Operations, CRM and Cinematography etc.

B-Plan Competition

Business plan competition had great opportunities to get funded for business ideas, get feedback on strategic plan, and exposure to the right audience.
