UpStart Conquest

Upstart conquest provides the platform where your startup can find its foundation. Apart from the capital, you can attract the attention and advice of the panel and not to forget, the immense networking, informal interaction and feedback from the judges.

Event Structure

  • Phase I: Submission Of Executive Summary
    •
  • All the documents should be converted to PDF and then mailed to divyansh@edcjss.in with SUBJECT:'Event Name' Executive Summary,Upstart conquest should be submitted in a single mail. Even if you want to submit any document later, attach all the documents in that mail.
  • Phase II: Final
    •
  • Closed door pitching in front of the reputed VC's and Angel Investors of India; 10-12 min presentation followed by Q And A.

GuideLines

  • All participants are required to fill the Executive Summary Form.
  • The Executive Summary should adhere to the format given and own versions should not be created. Participants not adhering to the format will be disqualified.
  • Please study the Example Executive Summary to get an idea of the information that the teams are required to pen down on the form.
  • A photo-identity proof has to be submitted for all the participants.
  • All the documents should be converted to PDF and then mailed to divyansh@edcjss.in should be submitted in a single mail.
  • Entries need to have an innovative value (an innovative idea provides a solution for a new need, or advancement compared to existing products, services or technologies).

Judging Criterias

  • The Originality of idea , The Feasibility , Scalabilty and overall completion capabilty of product.
  • Evidence of demand for the venture in the marketplace; market size and growth opportunity. The need of the product to society.
  • The potential for the venture to receive financial support from additional investors/donors
  • Identify risk factors associated with the enterprise and external environment. Develop a plan for mitigating them
  • Quantify venture leader's ability to develop people, promote change and achieve results.
